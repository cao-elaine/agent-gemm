--- Step 6: Alternative Strategies Considered ---

1. Smaller padded shape: Not possible. Mp must be >= 300; next ALLOWED_M below
   512 is 256, which is < 300. So Mp=512 is the minimum.

2. Larger padded shape for a "faster" tile: The profile entry for (512, 512, 1024)
   in bf16 would not be better — it pads K more (from 768 to 1024), increasing
   both padding cost and NPU compute time.

3. Different M padding: Could try Mp=1024 which allows tile (64,128,64) — but
   that tile fails even at 512x512x768 for bf16, and the larger padded shape
   would increase execution time substantially.

4. Tile (32, 64, 32): Working but slower at 442.555 us vs 351.881 us for (64,64,64).
