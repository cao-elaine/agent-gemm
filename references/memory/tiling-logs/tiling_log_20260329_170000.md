## Main Patterns Confirmed

1. **m=32 consistently beats m=16 for Mp=64 shapes**: Improvement 2–46% across all N/K combos
2. **m=32 marginally beats m=16 for Mp=32 shapes**: 1–7% improvement (NPU launch overhead dominates)
3. **Large-N shape optimal tiles**: (32,64,32) for Np=480–672; (32,64,32) or (32,16,64) for Np=960; (32,128,64) for Np=2560/3072
4. **m=64 tiles underperform m=32 tiles on most large-N shapes** (except K=32 shapes) — m=64 gives Pm=1 which may have less pipelining benefit
5. **m=64, n=16 combination crashes NPU hardware** on Np≥480 — avoid this combination
